Our Story

In the bustling streets of Mumbai, a dedicated physician specializing in clinical research, and a sharp software quality assurance professional, shared a daily ritual for over 15 years. Every morning and evening, they crammed into the same overcrowded roads, heading to their respective jobs in healthcare and IT. What started as small talk about traffic soon evolved into deep dives on healthcare's thorniest issues.


Ganesh would bluster about overburdened clinics, where patients waited hours for fragmented care, misdiagnoses from siloed data, and the grind of manual record-keeping in clinical practice. Ajay countered with tech insights, bugs in hospital software leading to errors, scalability woes in telemedicine apps, and untapped potential of AI for predictive diagnostics. They'd debate regulatory hurdles like FDA and CDSCO guidelines for AI devices, getting streamlined databases to train AI etc.


One crisp monsoon evening, after a decade and a half of this routine, something shifted. As the bus rattled past flooded streets, Ganesh sketched a vision: an all-in-one platform integrating patient records, physician notes, laboratory test names, pharmacy drug list etc. "Imagine if we fixed this mess," he said. Ajay's eyes lit up. "With my QA expertise and your clinical know-how, we could build it unbreakable, scalable, secure, transformative." By the time they stepped off, they had a name: HealthDesk, an app to deskill healthcare chaos.


Ytal Tech Consultancy and Services LLP was initiated with the vision to resolve chaos in healthcare management documentation and have streamlined path to access patient records and handle healthcare units in de-fragmented manner.

Our Products

HealthDesk app

Ytal Tech Solutions & Services LLP presents Healthdesk — a comprehensive healthcare app that connects patients, physicians, pharmacies, laboratories, hospitals, and pharma partners on a single platform to streamline care, communication, and administration.

  • Patient medical history: add comprehensive disease history, allergies, and past conditions
  • Appointments: schedule and manage appointments with registered physicians and clinics
  • Medicine refill & compliance: set medicine reminders, track refill needs, and monitor adherence
  • Role switching: toggle between patient, dependent, physician, pharmacy, laboratory, and hospital roles

OncoHealthDesk

OncoHealthDesk is a purpose-built oncology EMR for daycare settings developed by Ytal Tech Solutions & Services LLP, designed to streamline cancer care workflows while preserving clinical rigor and auditability.

  • Centralizes patient oncology records and automates routine oncology-specific tasks
  • Calculates oncology endpoints — progression-free survival (PFS) and overall survival (OS)
  • Built-in tumour board feature supports case presentation and consensus treatment plans

Integritee

Ytal Tech and Solutions presents Integritee — a 21 CFR Part 11–compliant platform purpose-built to modernize Ethics Committee (EC) and Institutional Review Board (IRB) workflows.

  • 21 CFR Part 11 compliance, with secure electronic signatures, audit trails, and tamper-evident records.
  • Automated workflow routing for submissions, reviews, revisions, approvals, and renewals.
  • Exportable, audit-ready reports and metadata to simplify inspections and regulatory submissions.
